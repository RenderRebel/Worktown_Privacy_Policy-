# Privacy Policy for Worktown **Last Updated: July 19, 2026** Worktown ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Worktown mobile application (the "App"), a real-time hyperlocal job matching platform that connects local workers with nearby service providers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using Worktown, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. --- ## 1. Information We Collect ### 1.1 Information You Provide - **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register. - **Profile Information:** Profile photo, skills, work experience, and mode preference (Worker or Provider). - **Job Information:** Job listings, applications, task descriptions, and pricing details you post or apply to. - **PIN Code / Location Data:** Your PIN code or area location, used to match you with nearby jobs or workers. - **Communications:** Messages, reviews, ratings, and any information you submit when contacting support. ### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically - **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app version. - **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, features used, job status interactions, and time spent in the App. - **Location Data:** Approximate or precise location (if permission granted) to enable hyperlocal job matching. - **Push Notification Tokens:** Used to deliver real-time job status updates (e.g., Pending, Accepted, Arriving, In Progress, Done, Completed). ### 1.3 Information from Third Parties - Data processed through **Cloud Firestore** and Firebase services (e.g., authentication, crash reporting, analytics, push notifications). --- ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - Match Workers and Providers based on hyperlocal PIN code data. - Facilitate job postings, applications, and real-time job lifecycle tracking. - Send push notifications and status updates via Cloud Firestore. - Verify user identity and maintain account security. - Improve, personalize, and troubleshoot the App. - Respond to customer support requests. - Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service. --- ## 3.
